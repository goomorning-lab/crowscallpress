Understanding Levels

Two pathways. One destination.

Students progress through levels aligned with ability, not just grade, ensuring every child is appropriately challenged and growing.

Crown Pathway Reading & Understanding

Emphasizes fluency, comprehension, and confidence in tackling texts.

Civic
A1–A2
Fluency, vocabulary-in-context, literal comprehension. Step Into Reading (Step 4).
Candidate
A1–A2
Summarizing chapters, character tracking, predictions. Magic Tree House & more.
Monarch
A2–B1
Genre familiarity, theme spotting, text-to-self connections.
Leaders Pathway Analysis & Critical Thinking

Encourages students to move beyond comprehension into interpretation, argumentation, and critical thinking.

Junior
A2–B1
Claim + Evidence basics, literary elements, discussion norms.
Lexis
B1–B2
Author craft, figurative language, cross-text connections.
Mythos
B2–C1
Interpretive theses, rhetoric, genre conventions. Close reading.
Opsis
C1+
Critical lenses, comparative analysis, synthesis writing.
