Beyond the Workbook

Other curriculum & workbooks.

We offer a variety of supplementary programs and workbooks designed to stretch students in different directions, from critical thinking and rhetoric to vocabulary and imaginative play.

Ask Us About These
01
Roleplaying Game
An immersive, story-driven curriculum that builds reading comprehension, creative writing, and collaborative thinking through structured RPG play.
02
News Analysis
Students engage with real-world news to develop media literacy, critical reading, and the ability to identify bias, argument structure, and evidence.
03
Greek & Latin Roots
A vocabulary-building workbook that teaches the building blocks of English through roots, prefixes, and suffixes from classical languages.
04
Debate ⭐ Popular
Our most sought-after program. Students learn structured argumentation, rebuttal techniques, and confident public speaking through formal and informal debate formats.
